FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

-

What is the price of Kia Seltos?

Kia Seltos MSRP* starts at Rs. 10.49 Lakh (exclusive of all taxes).

What is the price of Kia Seltos top model?

Kia Seltos MSRP* for the top model starts at Rs.16.99 Lakh (exclusive of all taxes).

Does the Kia Seltos come with a sunroof?

Yes, an electric sunroof is offered in 5 variants of the Kia Seltos car that is HTX, HTX+, GTX(O), GTX+, and X Line.

What are the seating options available in the Kia Seltos?

The seating capacity of Kia Seltos car is 5.

How many airbags are there in Kia Seltos?

In Kia Seltos 6 airbags are offered as standard across all variants.

What are the transmission options available in the Kia Seltos?

The Kia Seltos is available in both manual and automatic transmissions, namely – 7DCT, 6AT, 6 MT, IVT/iMT.
