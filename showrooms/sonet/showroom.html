FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

-

What is the price of Kia Sonet?

Kia Sonet MSRP* starts at Rs. 7.49 Lakh (exclusive of all taxes).

What is the price of Kia Sonet top model?

Kia Sonet MSRP* for the top model starts at Rs. 12.59 Lakh (exclusive of all taxes).

What are the seating options available in the Kia Sonet?

The seating capacity of Kia Sonet car is 5.

Does the Kia Sonet come with a sunroof?

Yes, an electric sunroof is offered in 4 variants of the Kia Sonet car - HTX, HTX AE, HTX+, and GTX+.

How many airbags are there in Kia Sonet?

Kia Sonet offers 4 airbags as standard and an option of 6 airbags in select variants.

What are the transmission options available in the Kia Sonet?

The Kia Sonet is available in both manual and automatic transmissions, namely - 7DCT, 6AT, iMT and 5 MT.
